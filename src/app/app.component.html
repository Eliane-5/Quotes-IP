About Us

This app was mainly structured to help upcoming artists who aspire to be poets.
We thought it would be a good thing for them to have a good place where they could
start practicing and go public with their amazing quotes. This app will achieve
that by helping them first go public with their quotes, which would help them get
reviews by the numbers of likes the quote can have.
With that they could do better next time.
But this could also be a good place for you quote lovers who can post whatever quote u love.

Enjoy!
